    I am a final year Computer Science Ph.D. student at Pennsylvania State University.

    My Ph.D. research covers Algorithms for Bioinformatics, Information Retrieving of Big-Data, and Machine Learning.

    I design algorithms and tools for human genomic variants, and transcriptome data analysis.
    I am lucky to have Paul Medvedev as my advisor.

    I have strong interest in biomedical science, which is the cornerstone of human health and happiness. And I believe computational methods are promising tech for solving biomedical problems.

    I also have a M.S. degree in Bioinformatics from University of Chinese Academy of Sciences.
